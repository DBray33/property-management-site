Common Questions

Renter FAQs

Rent can be paid online through your tenant portal via credit card, debit card, or ACH transfer. Payments are processed securely and you'll receive a confirmation email.

Log into your tenant portal and submit a request with details and photos. For emergencies, call our 24/7 emergency line at 123-456-7890.

Complete our online application which includes a background check, credit check, income verification, and rental history review. We typically process applications within 48 hours.

Pet policies vary by property. Check the listing details or contact us to ask about pet policies for a specific property.

We'll contact you 60 days before your lease expires to discuss renewal options. If you choose to move out, we'll guide you through the move-out process and security deposit return.
