Our Services

At Property Management Group, we offer a comprehensive range of services to ensure your property is well-managed and profitable. Our services cater to both property owners and renters, providing everything you need for a seamless property management experience.

Marketing

We use effective marketing strategies to attract quality tenants quickly. Our team ensures your property is showcased in the best light.

Tenant Screening

Our thorough tenant screening process includes background checks, credit checks, and rental history verification to find reliable tenants.

Rent Collection

We handle rent collection efficiently, ensuring timely payments and managing any late payment issues professionally.

Maintenance

Our team coordinates all maintenance and repair requests, keeping your property in excellent condition and addressing issues promptly.

Accounting

We provide detailed financial reports and tax documentation, ensuring transparency and helping you stay on top of your property’s finances.

Eviction

Should eviction become necessary, we handle the process professionally and in compliance with state and local laws.

Lease Management

We manage all aspects of lease agreements, from drafting to renewals and terminations, ensuring compliance and reducing your administrative burden.

24/7 Support

Our team provides round-the-clock support for both you and your tenants, addressing emergencies and urgent issues promptly.
