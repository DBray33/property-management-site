Our Story

Local Roots, Professional Standards.

Founded in Reading, PA, Property Management Group was built on a simple idea: property management should be transparent, responsive, and fair — for owners and tenants alike.

We saw too many property owners struggling with the day-to-day demands of managing rentals, and too many tenants dealing with unresponsive management. PMG was created to bridge that gap with technology-driven solutions, genuine care, and local expertise.

Today, we manage a growing portfolio of residential, multi-family, commercial, and short-term rental properties across the greater Reading area. Our team brings together decades of real estate experience with modern tools to deliver an experience that sets the standard for property management in our region.
